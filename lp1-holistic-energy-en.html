What is the difference between holistic and conventional massage? +
Conventional massage primarily addresses physical muscle tension. Holistic energy massage works simultaneously on physical, emotional, and energetic layers — addressing not just the symptom (tension) but its root cause (blockage in the energy field).
Does the session involve any sexual activity? +
No. All sessions are strictly therapeutic and energetic. The work focuses on releasing blockages, restoring energy flow, and promoting genuine well-being. The environment is professional, safe, and respectful at all times.
How does pricing work? Can I get a quote? +
Sessions are priced based on the modality and duration chosen. To receive a personalised quote with full details, simply send a message via WhatsApp — Mari will respond with all the information you need within a short time.
Where do sessions take place? +
All sessions take place in Mari's private studio in Amsterdam. The address and exact directions are shared upon booking confirmation.
What should I wear to the session? +
Wear comfortable, easy-to-remove clothing such as loose trousers and a simple top. Please arrive already showered if possible — a shower is available on-site as well.
Are there any contraindications? +
Sessions are not recommended for those with fever, deep vein thrombosis, fractures, severe osteoporosis, open wounds, or high-risk pregnancy. If you have any health concerns, please mention them when booking.
